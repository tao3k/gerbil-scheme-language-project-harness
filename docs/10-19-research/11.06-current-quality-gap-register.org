* Scope

This register is the current engineering-quality ledger for the Gerbil Scheme
language project harness.  It supersedes stale command receipts embedded in
research audits and keeps the next implementation loop tied to live provider
surfaces.

The status terms are:

- =closed=: behavior is implemented and covered by command/test evidence.
- =partial=: behavior exists, but search, docs, tests, or closure still drift.
- =open=: research describes the need, but the harness does not yet expose it.
- =deferred=: the change is intentionally not made until policy evidence asks
  for it.
